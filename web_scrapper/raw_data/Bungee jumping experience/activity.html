Localbird Experience

Bungee jumping local experience

The aim of this experience is to connect local communities and businesses with international eco-travelers.

The  time, the place and the date can change  because  weather condition  or  minimum amount of people , please join our WhatsApp group after purchasing the experience so we can confirm your participation.

Information

  • Joining the WhatsApp group for last-minute communications is mandatory
  • We are not responsible for lost information if a participant does not join the group
  • We can’t confirm the experience until the minimum group number is reached, in order to maintain the special price
  • It’s not possible to book less than 24 hours before the event

The Experience

Join us for this exciting and thrilling adventure of Bungee jumping! This activity has developed and improved a lot recently. The current safety systems are far superior to those used a short time ago. The double calculation of the jump has made this activity safe. An experienced team will guide you through the local environment and teach you how to respect it and appreciate it at its best.

Included

  • Insurance
  • bungee jumping activities
  • safety tools
  • photo

  • The experience depends on the environmental conditions, organizers take the right to change the experiences according to the group security
  • Only those who pay through the event link will have his/her place in the event.
  • All equipment will be provided, so you don’t have to worry about a thing.
